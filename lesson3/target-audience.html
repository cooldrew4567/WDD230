Target Audience Description

The target audience of Placer Chamber of Commerce description is as follow:

  • Who:Members of a Chamber of Commerce are usually made of local buisnesses who love their city/county
  • Age: Must be 18 and up.
  • Technology: The main audience will most likely use cell phones since they are such a big thing now. Computers will also be used and user friendly to the site.
  • Motivation: Chambers of Commerce main activities are to gather everyone they can together to help local buisnesses and give them that push they need
